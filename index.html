About Me Translation Editing Writing UX Design

Hello, my name is Anna Andersen.

I’m a writer, editor, translator, and ux designer based in Austin, Texas.

I grew up in San Diego, California. Shortly after graduating from UCLA with a bachelor’s in communication studies, I moved to Reykjavik, Iceland, where I completed a master’s in journalism and worked as a journalist and culture magazine editor for many years.

Upon moving back to the States I grew increasingly interested in ux design, and I realized that my background in journalism, including strong research, interview, and storytelling skills, was especially well suited to the field. I decided to take an intensive, six-month ux design course through The University of Texas McCombs School of Business, learned the technical skills, and things took off from there.

Looking for writing, editing, translation, or design help? Drop me a line!
